## üéØ Quick Reference

### **Core Pillars**
1. **Authenticity** - Realistic police work, no fantasy elements
2. **Autonomy** - Player-driven investigation, minimal hand-holding
3. **Analysis** - Deep document reading and evidence correlation
4. **Patience** - Real-time forensics, deliberate pacing

### **Key Features**
- ‚úÖ Static document investigation (PDFs, photos)
- ‚úÖ Real-time forensic analysis system
- ‚úÖ Multiple suspects with complex motives
- ‚úÖ Solution submission with limited attempts
- ‚úÖ Detective ranking progression
- ‚ùå NO mini-games or action sequences
- ‚ùå NO dialogue trees or interrogations
- ‚ùå NO timer pressure or arcade elements
- ‚ùå NO fantasy/supernatural elements

### **Target Experience**
> "You're a cold case detective. You have documents, photos, forensic reports, and time. No shortcuts, no magic clues. Just your brain and determination."
